After the deadline, any accepted paper without a registered REGISTRANT will be set to NO-SHOW status — not included in the Final Program and not submitted to IEEE Xplore Library.
1
One Registrant per paper
At least one author per accepted paper must register as REGISTRANT before the deadline.
2
Declare a Presenter
At least one author must be declared as PRESENTER. Papers without oral presentation are set to NO-SHOW.
3
Presenter must be registered
Every PRESENTER must be registered as either ATTENDEE or REGISTRANT.
4
Up to three presentations
Each registered author (as ATTENDEE or REGISTRANT) may present up to three works.
